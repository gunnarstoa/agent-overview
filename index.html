Delivery Prep & Quality

PSMs ensure delivery teams are fully prepared before project kickoff and maintain quality oversight throughout. This includes pre-project enablement sessions, solutioning reviews with the partner team, and ongoing delivery health checks to protect CSAT scores.

PRE-PROJECT
  • › Team skill assessment
  • › Enablement gap analysis
  • › Solutioning review
DURING DELIVERY
  • › Weekly quality check-ins
  • › Escalation management
  • › Delivery milestone tracking
POST-PROJECT
  • › CSAT collection
  • › Lessons learned
  • › Capability updates
Executive Engagement

PSMs facilitate strategic alignment between partner executives and Anaplan leadership. This includes co-selling motions, executive sponsorship alignment, and ensuring partner leadership has visibility into pipeline and delivery performance.

KEY ACTIVITIES
  • › Client project oversight
  • › Co-selling & exec alignment
  • › QBR executive summaries
  • › Strategic account planning
Early Release Programs

PSMs coordinate partner access to beta features and early releases, capturing structured feedback that flows back to product teams. This creates a virtuous cycle: partners gain competitive advantage, and Anaplan gets real-world validation before GA.

KEY ACTIVITIES
  • › Beta access coordination
  • › Partner feedback loops to product
  • › Roadmap influence & advocacy
  • › Early adopter enablement
Partner Applications

PSMs evaluate and onboard strategic partners through a structured application process, ensuring new partners have the right capabilities, market focus, and commitment level to succeed in the ecosystem.

KEY ACTIVITIES
  • › Strategic partner evaluation
  • › Onboarding white-glove partners
  • › Capability assessment
  • › Market fit analysis
Delivery Readiness

PSMs assess and ensure partner teams are fully prepared to deliver before projects begin, including certification verification, team composition review, and solutioning alignment.

KEY ACTIVITIES
  • › Pre-project team enablement
  • › Solutioning reviews
  • › Certification verification
  • › Team composition assessment
Content Enablement

PSMs curate and distribute enablement resources to partners, keeping the partner portal up to date and ensuring partners have access to the latest playbooks, templates, and best practices.

KEY ACTIVITIES
  • › Curate & share resources
  • › Partner portal updates
  • › Playbook maintenance
  • › Best practice distribution
Proactive Comms

PSMs drive proactive communication to partners including release notes, product updates, training reminders, and event notifications — keeping partners informed and engaged without requiring 1:1 outreach.

KEY ACTIVITIES
  • › Release notes & partner updates
  • › Training reminders
  • › Event notifications
  • › Newsletter curation
